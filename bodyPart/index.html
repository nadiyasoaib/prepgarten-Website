Human Body Parts

Head

The head is the top part of the body. It has eyes, nose, mouth, and ears.

Eyes

The eyes are the organs that help us see.

Nose

The nose is the organ that helps us smell.

Mouth

The mouth is the organ that helps us eat and talk.

Ears

The ears are the organs that help us hear.

Neck

The neck is the part of the body that connects the head to the body.

Shoulders

The shoulders are the parts of the body that connect the arms to the body.

Arms

The arms are the parts of the body that help us move and do things.

Hands

The hands are the parts of the body that help us grasp and manipulate objects.

Leg

The legs Keep the body stable and help in walking & running.

Hair

The hair Protects against external factors.

Teeth

Teeth help us to bite and chew our food.
